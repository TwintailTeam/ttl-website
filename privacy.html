Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Twintail Launcher ("TTL", "the launcher", "we") is built with privacy as a first principle. This page explains, plainly, what the launcher does and does not do with your data.

What we collect

Nothing. The launcher does not collect or log any telemetry, analytics, usage statistics or any other type of personal data — and it never will. Your installed games, settings and play activity stay entirely on your own machine.

Network connections

To download and update games the launcher connects to the relevant game distribution servers and to our public manifest repositories. These connections are necessary to fetch files and version information; we do not attach any personally identifying data to them.

Third-party games

Games installed and launched through TTL are developed and operated by their respective publishers. Those games may collect information according to their own privacy policies. That data collection is outside our control and is not something the launcher manages or has access to.

Open source

TTL is fully open source under the GPL-3.0 license. You are welcome to inspect exactly what the launcher does at any time on GitHub.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out via our GitHub issues or our Discord server.
